Emergency Rescue for Stray Dogs

Every minute matters. We respond to emergency calls for injured, sick, and abandoned stray dogs and provide immediate medical care.

How Our Rescue Process Works

📞 Rescue Call

We receive emergency rescue calls from the community.

🚑 On-Site Rescue

Our rescue team reaches the location and safely rescues the dog.

🏥 Medical Care

The dog receives urgent treatment, surgery, or medication.

💙 Recovery & Care

The dog is nurtured until fully recovered and safe.
